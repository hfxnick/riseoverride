Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

At Override Labs, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect and use your data through the RiseOverride Chrome Extension and our website, overridelabs.ca.

1. What We Collect

We do not collect personal data by default. If you use Pro Mode, submit community content, or send feedback, you may voluntarily provide your email address. This is only used for licensing, communication, or support purposes.

2. Chrome Extension Permissions

  • downloads: Allows SCORM file interception and patching.
  • scripting: Enables code injection into downloaded content.
  • storage: Saves your custom code locally in your browser.
  • host_permissions: Detects SCORM exports from articulate-us.s3.amazonaws.com.

3. Pro Mode Licensing & Payments

Pro Mode requires an email address for license management. Subscription payments are processed via Stripe. We do not store or process payment details‚ÄîStripe (and its subsidiaries, including LemonSqueezy) handle this according to their own privacy policy.

In preparation for our upcoming migration to LemonSqueezy licensing, we may collect your license number and email address through a secure form to ensure uninterrupted access. This information is only used to verify and transfer your existing Pro Mode access.

4. Website Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics on our website (overridelabs.ca) to understand traffic patterns. This data is anonymized and used solely for internal insights. No tracking occurs inside the RiseOverride Chrome Extension itself.

5. Community Submissions

If you submit a custom CSS style for inclusion in the Community Style Library, we require your email address as part of the Google Forms submission process. This is a platform requirement and is only used to contact you about your submission and grant free Pro Mode access if approved.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as new features are released. Check back regularly for the most recent version.
